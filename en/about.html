About Me

A brief introduction

I'm a robotics engineer passionate about embedded systems, control algorithms, and robot competitions. My work spans from low-level STM32 firmware development to high-level robot control systems using ROS2 and MuJoCo simulation.

As a member of the RoboMaster team, I've developed embedded control frameworks for various robot types including omnidirectional chassis, gimbal systems, and shooting mechanisms. I enjoy bridging the gap between hardware and software.
